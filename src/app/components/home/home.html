School District of Philadelphia District-Wide Surveys

Each year, the Office of Research and Evaluation (ORE) invites Philadelphia District and Charter students, parents/guardians, teachers, and principals to participate in a survey about their schools.

The survey feedback provides schools with stakeholder perspectives on what is working and what areas need to be improved.

Exploring the Results

Start exploring the data by selecting one of the categories below. “Response Rate” will take you to a page where you can view how many students, parents/guardians, teachers, and principals took the survey.

For responses directly related to the District’s Anchor Goals, choose the “Highlights” icon. “Topics” will tell you more about the five main survey topics and display a report that shows topic scores. “Question Explorer” allows you to choose a question and explore the findings. “About” provides background information about the survey.

As you review this information, you may want to focus on response rates, responses across the key survey topics (e.g., "school climate"), or responses to individual questions (including select focus questions tied to the District’s Anchor Goals). Use the green selection bar on the top of your screen to make school, network, and other student group selections.
