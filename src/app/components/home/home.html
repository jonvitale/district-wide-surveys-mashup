Philadelphia School Surveys

Each year, Philadelphia public and charter school students (grades 3+), parents, teachers, and principals are invited to participate in a survey about their schools.

The feedback helps schools identify what is working and what areas need to be improved based on information collected directly from school stakeholders.

Exploring the Results

The survey results are displayed in several ways. First, you can view how many people took the survey. This is important because with more survey participants it is more likely that the information accurately reflects the situation at a given school. The results are presented in charts and tables in order to make comparisons and see year-to-year changes in how people responded to the survey questions.

As you review information on the surveys, you may want to focus on response rates, responses to individual questions (including selected focus questions tied to district anchor goals), and major topics (e.g., "school climate").
