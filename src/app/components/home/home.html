The School District of Philadelphia

District-Wide Surveys

Each year all School District of Philadelphia parents, teachers, principals, and students (grades 3+) are invited to participate in a survey about their school. This feedback helps identify what is working well and what areas need to be improved.

Viewing the Results

The results are displayed in charts and tables to make comparisons and year-to-year changes easy to read and understand. As you view the results you may focus on response rate, responses to individual questions (including selected focus questions), and major topics (e.g., "school climate"). Click one of the following to navigate to the data.

How do students, parents, teachers, and principals respond to all questions? Compare schools, networks, or groups within schools.
