How was each question answered?

The District-Wide Survey includes approximately {{$ctrl.numQuestionsStudent}} student questions, {{$ctrl.numQuestionsParent}} parent questions, {{$ctrl.numQuestionsTeacher}} teacher questions, and {{$ctrl.numQuestionsPrincipal}} principal questions. On this page you can view aggregated responses to each of these questions. For a list of all questions included on the 2019 surveys, visit the Survey Questions website.

Click the button, "⊕ Select a new question" to view questions based on respondent type, topic, and sub-topics. After choosing a question, the chart at right displays the % of respondents who selected each possible response (bars display data for {{$ctrl.CYTD}} while circles show the same data {{$ctrl.PYTD}}). Blue bars and circles represent the "most positive" response, while red bars and circles represent the "least positive" response. You may hover over a bar to see the numbers for each year.

In the blue ribbons below you can compare responses to specific questions by respondent charecteristics,  by locatation, or across networks and schools.
