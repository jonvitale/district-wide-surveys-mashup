What are the main topics in the surveys?

After analyzing survey results the Office of Research and Evaluation categorized each question into one of five general topics. For each topic a score is constructed by calculating the % of responses to topic-related questions that are the best possible choice. On this page you can choose a topic to compare schools and other groups of students for each of these topics.

School Climate - Questions addressing bullying, safety, attendance, discipline, and relationships
Instruction - Questions addressing teaching, student engagement, data usage, and district support
Parent/Guardian-Community Ties - Questions addressing outreach, communication quality, guardian involvement, and networks (Parent, Teacher, and Principal only)
School Leadership - Questions addressing leadership decisions, feedback, and school focus priorities (Teacher and Principal only)
Professional Capacity - Questions addressing peer collaboration, professional development, and opportunites for innovation (Teacher and Principal only)

Select a respondent group and topic to view the average topic score by school on the map. You can zoom or scroll the area of the map

Select a respondent group and topic to view the average topic score by network. If a network is selected, schools within that network will be displayed. If a school is selected, grades may be displayed.
